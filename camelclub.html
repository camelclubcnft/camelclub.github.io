What rights do I get when I own a Camel Club NFT?

When you purchase a Camel Club NFT either directly from us or on the secondary market, you are the owner of that NFT. This grants you personal and commercial copyright the specific NFT to copy, display and distribute with no revenue limits.

However, this does not allow you to use Camel Club trademark, logo, or any Camel Club intellectual property.
